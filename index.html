About Our Farm

Hi, we're Amy and H.R. Reinke, running A + H Urban Farm together with our three wonderful children. As a family-run operation in Oklahoma, we specialize in naturally grown seasonal produce, sustainably grown seasonal cut flowers, and pastured pork from our pigs.

We focus on regenerative and sustainable practices—like rotating our pigs through oak-filled forested areas to forage naturally and clear land—while building toward a self-sufficient farmstead. We're passionate about providing fresh, high-quality, chemical-free food and beautiful flowers straight from our family farm to your table, supporting local families and community.
