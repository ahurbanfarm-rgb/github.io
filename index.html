About Our Farm

Hi, I'm Amy Reinke, a market farmer based in Oklahoma. At A + H Urban Farm, we specialize in naturally grown seasonal produce, fresh vegetables, vibrant cut flowers, and farm-raised goods like eggs and more. Our family-run operation focuses on sustainable practices, including rotating our pigs through oak-filled forested areas to naturally forage and clear land.

We aim to build a self-sufficient farmstead that provides a whole diet for over 100 families, reducing grocery costs while supporting local jobs. From pumpkins and peppers to homemade soups and beyond, we're passionate about bringing fresh, high-quality products straight from our farm to your table.
